## 📂 Project Components

### 1. Canva Presentation Design
Focused on visual communication, I created a Canva presentation that balances technical depth with aesthetic clarity. Moving beyond simple bullet points, I applied the "Rule of Thirds" and a consistent color palette to convey complex engineering concepts effectively. This exercise emphasized that well-designed visuals are essential for any engineer to pitch ideas or explain technical architectures to a diverse audience.

### 2. Portfolio
This section details the creation of a professional portfolio that serves as a central hub for my academic and personal achievements at VIT Bhopal. Designed for easy navigability, it showcases my progression from basic Python to complex systems like Linux/WSL. By curating my GitHub contributions and certifications in one space, I’ve developed the ability to present my skills in a professional, student-focused manner.

### 3. Platforms & Technical Tools
I utilize a specific digital ecosystem—including GitHub for version control and LinkedIn for networking—to bridge the gap between classroom theory and real-world application. This component highlights my experience with WSL (Windows Subsystem for Linux) and specialized platforms like "The Odin Project." Integrating these tools has created a streamlined workflow for managing code, tracking learning, and staying updated with global tech trends.

### 4. Cybercrime Prevention & Financial Safety
This section bridges professional decorum with digital defense. It covers Email Etiquette, focusing on formal salutations and high-impact subject lines for faculty and industry reach-outs. Simultaneously, it provides a rigorous framework for Cyber Safety, featuring an 8-point checklist of "Do's"—like verifying receiver names and enabling 2FA—and "Don’ts"—such as never entering a PIN to receive money or clicking unsolicited "job offer" links. Together, these guidelines ensure a secure and professional presence in the Indian digital landscape.

### 5. Case Study & Prevention Checklist
This section concludes with a practical case study on social engineering and a 100–150 word reflection on the psychological tactics used in modern UPI scams. By analyzing how scammers exploit the high-trust environment of Indian digital payments, I have developed an 8-point prevention checklist that serves as a tactical guide for students. This component emphasizes proactive habits, such as identifying "Request Money" fraud and verifying QR codes, while providing the official reporting protocols for the National Cyber Crime Portal and the 1930 Helpline.
